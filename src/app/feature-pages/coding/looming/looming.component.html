Synopsis:
Starting from a small disagreement, college roommates Quis & Tre get into an intense argument, which has never happened before in their five-year friendship. While built-up emotions towards one another are revealed, the true strength of their friendship is also tested. This dispute leads to both men figuring out how their relationship factors in their lives.
