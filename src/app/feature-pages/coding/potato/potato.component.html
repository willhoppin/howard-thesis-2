SHORT FILM
Hot Potato
Spring 2022
1 Episode
Dir. Mykel Ratliff-Brown
Prod. Eden Ann Anderson
Synopsis:
Hot Potato follows a young woman and senior in college Ashley who must face the perils of dating again once her relationship is in shambles. This short film is for anyone out there who has longed to find that 'one' amidst a college campu as well as a story of closing old doors and opening new chapters while tugging at your heart strings and tickling your funny bone.
