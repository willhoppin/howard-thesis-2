Over The Influence
Spring 2023
1 Episode
Dir. Dennis Simmons
Prod. Chesney Green
Synopsis:
Over the Influence is a psychological thriller that takes place the night before an important family dinner. A young man and his group of friends decide to gather at his apartment and do a few drugs in celebration of the holiday. Going through each stage of being high, he realizes there's more to life than it seems.
